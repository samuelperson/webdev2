Stir Fried Gyoza with Pork Bao

This dish is made all by hand and definitely not frozen. After being lightly seared with olive oil and put in a steam bath, this flavorfful Gyoza can be a good filler to get through the rest of the day. They are topped with green onion and with a side of soy sauce for dipping and pork bao buns to fill you up!
